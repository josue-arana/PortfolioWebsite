About Me
Past

I was born and raised in Guatemala City, Guatemala. My passion for art started when I was 8 years old. At age 18, I did my first full canvas drawing like the ones in my portfolio. In 2017, I obtained my Associate of Arts at Montgomery College, then transferred to the University of Maryland at College Park.

Present

Currently pursuing my bachelor in Computer Science. In the last couple of years, drawing has become more like a hobby. However, I've managed to pass on my creativity, passion, and dedication to excel in my field of study.

Future

My goal is to graduate, and become a Software Engineer to be able to work and learn from the best companies so that someday I can have my own company.
